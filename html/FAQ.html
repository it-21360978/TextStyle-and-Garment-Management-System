FAQ

How Long Will it Take to Get My Packege?

Your order will be packed on the next business day and the delivery will take place within 3-7 working days, also we provide a fast one day (24/7) delivery service around Colombo . Please check our Shipping Policy for further details.

Do I Need A Account To Place Order?

No you don’t need an account to place orders, but you will only be able to get reward points and track your orders upon your singing up to our website

Delivery Chargers?

Above mention all prices are change according to the weight. This prices are standard price’s (Less than weight 1KG)and if you try to order large size product please check delivery chargers through our 24/7 customer service.

How Do I Track My Order?

After logging into your account, the status of your checkout history can be found under Order History. For orders via registered postage, a order number (article tracking number) will be given to you after the receipt given from our website

How Get My receipt?

You will see a display of the information submitted to Our website. This is your online receipt. On this page, you can view a printable version and email the information. A copy of this information will be emailed to the email address that was given for your account.
