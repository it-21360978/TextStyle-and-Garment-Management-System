Your Personal Information

1) What We Collect

To improve the consumer experience when using our website we are collecting personal information which includes information such as:

2) Why we collect these information

The above-mentioned details are collected from you to manage your user account properly by preventing unauthorized access to your account and these information for us as a company is valuable in order to provide the user a personalized and tailored service experience and assistance. To prevent identity theft and other frauds we will be using there information for protect both the user and the company. Certain security measure has been taken in order to protect the account details and the user. To prevent breach of an account it is advised that the user does not share account details with external parties.
